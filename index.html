The Except One is a fun and exciting dice game that can be played with two players. The objective of the game is to be the first player to reach a score of 100 points.

How to Play:

  1. Each player takes turns rolling a dice.
  2. On your turn, click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice.
  3. The number rolled on the dice will be added to your current score.
  4. If you roll a 1, your current score will be reset to 0 and it will be the next player's turn.
  5. You can choose to "Hold" your current score and add it to your total score.
  6. The first player to reach the target score which is 100 wins the game.
